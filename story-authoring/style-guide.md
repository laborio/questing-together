# Style Guide (Template)

Use this file to keep tone and readability consistent.

## Tone
- Light fantasy, social mystery, low-combat emphasis.
- Clear, concise, mobile-friendly sentences.
- Avoid modern slang unless intentional.

## Voice
- Narration: grounded, sensory, and short.
- Dialogue: characterful, short lines.

## Pacing
- Scenes should fit on a phone screen without scrolling too much.
- 1-2 paragraphs for intro, 1-2 dialogue lines, then player actions.

## Stakes
- Each scene should reveal something or create a new tension.
